Getting started

Cryptocurrency is based on blockchain technology. Blockchain is literally a chain of blocks, but what makes it revolutionary is that each block of data only has one owner. Only allowing a block to have one owner makes transactions easier and monetary piracy harder. The blockchain itself is public, and stored on more than one device, making it hard to mess with the data. Cryptocurrency is gained when your computer's processing unit solves a complex math problem, and then adds a block to the chain.
